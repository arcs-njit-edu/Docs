# RCAB Workflow and Responsibilities

## Meetings
The committee will meet quarterly or as needed to submit proposals to outside agencies, review allocation proposals and discuss matters related to resource management.

## Reporting
The RCAB will report to the Faculty Senate and provide annual summaries of resource usage, and policy updates.

## Initial Duties
If approved, the interim committee will as the first order of business setup the governance. 

* Establish the procedure for individual colleges to appoint members to the committee.
* Investigate and establish policies or guidelines for equitable distribution of HPC resources.
